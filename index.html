Welcome to Roshee

Changing How Companies Do Business Together

Roshee is a web-based B2B purchasing platform that connects buyers and vendors. Roshee enables companies to manage every step of the corporate purchasing process in one place. Whether you're dealing with a single vendor or setting up a competitive bidding process, Roshee helps you get all the decision-makers on one page.

Make better decisions together as a cohesive buying team

• With one click, you can invite colleagues to provide input on a purchase. Use your colleagues' expertise and make better deals for your company.
• Anyone on your buying team can see the full deal history in one place. Increased transparency helps your team identify important issues early and results in better outcomes.
• With Roshee's built-in project management features, you can assign action items to the vendor or to members of your buying team. More accountability and a smoother workflow results in a hassle-free process.
