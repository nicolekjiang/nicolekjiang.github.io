Why Hack?

uWaterloo students hacking for the Waterloo community! Express your creativity and build off of concepts and ideas you have formed in your classes and personal interests. Join the hackathon to show your skills in programming, design, planning, art, mathematics, engineering, science, health, technology, environment and your passion for making a difference amongst your peers.


Hacking is an incredible learning experience and a great way to network in your community and build something from the ground up. Oh and we have some great sponsors providing food, prizes and swag to ensure you have an awesome time!

Join the Hack now!

Rules

The base for this hackathon is promoting Waterloo pride, uWaterloo students working together, building projects to either help students or anyone in general. The idea is to come with an open mind and create something to show your peers how much you’ve learned from your work here.


Participants must either begin working on a new project from scratch, or write an independent module for a larger piece of existing software. If the latter is chosen, only the module itself will be taken into consideration for judging. When making a submission, the participant must also chose ‘module’ as their project type.

Mentors and Volunteers

We are looking for volunteers who want to experience the hackathon but don’t necessarily want to compete for the prize. If you have never been to a hackathon and want the full experience of music, coding, designing, energy drinks and great people sign up to be a volunteer for hack uwaterloo. Be a part of something revolutionary!


Mentors needed! Often times during hackathons, a group will hit a roadblock because they have an AMAZING idea and no way of implementing it. We would love to have students who have hackathon experience to come be student support for the teams in need. Lecturers and assistants are welcome to sign up as well! Watch your students apply concepts to the real world!

FAQ

What prizes are available?

Cool swags from our sponsors; Various raffle prizes to give out; Monetary prizes will be awarded to winners for various judging categories

What program do I have to be in?

Any student in any Waterloo program/faculty plan is welcome to participate in the hackathon!

Do I have to have prior programming experience?

Prior programming experience would be an asset. However, anyone who is motivated to learn, create and solve problems is encouraged to attend. If you're inexperienced in software development and think you might be overwhelmed by the competition - don't worry, a hackathon is an opportunity for person growth. You may benefit from collaboration with others. It's the experience that matters!

What should I bring?

You should bring your idea, enthusiasm, and your devices for work

How big can my team be?

You can have maximum 4 team members on your team.

Must I have a team?

You don't have to have a team, however collaboration is definitely encouraged. We should love to see cool developement and design work in your product and teamwork would often helps! If you would like to join a team, be sure to identify your role and what roles you're looking for in the sign up form.

Will there be food provided at the event?

We will be able to cover essential meals and caffeine during the event! Be sure to let us know your dietary restrictions so we can accommodate your conditions.
